## About Caldera

Caldera is an automated adversary emulation platform designed to support cybersecurity professionals and organizations. It enables users to:

- Simulate advanced persistent threats (APTs) quickly and safely within their own environments.
- Evaluate security postures by seeing how their defense mechanisms detect, respond, and recover from real-world simulated attacks.
- Experiment and train on a flexible and extensible open-source framework.

MITRE Caldera helps bridge the gap between offensive and defensive operations for a holistic approach to cybersecurity.
